Delivery Policy - Gorgia Online Store

These delivery terms apply only to the Gorgia online store.
During promotional periods, delivery terms may change, and updated information will be displayed on relevant website pages.

---
Definitions:
Buyer, Seller, and Customer refer to both individuals and legal entities.

---
General Conditions:
- Gorgia delivers online store purchases throughout Georgia.
- Orders placed after 18:00 are processed the next day.
- Orders placed on Sunday are processed on Monday.
- The seller must process the customer’s order within 14 business days after payment confirmation.
- Delivery must be completed within 2 business days after order processing unless exceptions apply.

Exceptions:
1. If a product is made to order, production takes 10–14 business days, and delivery 4–5 additional days.
2. If the product is not available in Tbilisi branches, it will be transferred from other branches, and delivery will take up to 14 business days.
3. During large promotional periods (e.g. Gorgia’s Birthday, Black Friday, New Year), delivery may take up to 10 business days after processing.

Free Delivery:
- Orders over 500 GEL are delivered free in Tbilisi, Batumi, Kutaisi, Zugdidi, Telavi, and Marneuli.
- Free delivery and carrying for doors, furniture, and appliances from 250 GEL within Tbilisi only (price per item must be 250 GEL).

---
Notes:
- If Gorgia’s logistics team agrees on the delivery date with the customer one day in advance and contacts them one hour before arrival, but the customer does not accept the order, free re-delivery is not available. The customer must pay for re-delivery or use self-pickup.
- Providing a personal identification number is mandatory when placing an order. Without it, the order cannot be processed.

---
Self-Pickup Rules:
- The maximum self-pickup period is 1 month. After that, the order is canceled, and the paid amount remains on the customer’s account at Gorgia.
- Self-pickup is available until 19:00, after the sales manager confirms the order is ready.

---
Delivery Fees:
- Fees are automatically calculated on the checkout page.
- Delivery is made only to the specified address. Unloading and carrying inside are not included.
- Customers can choose between delivery or free store pickup.

Full consumer agreement is available on the website.
