Delivery Terms

On-Site Delivery Service

When purchasing products on the Zoomer website, customers can use the on-site delivery service.

Delivery Options – Within Tbilisi, customers can choose between standard and express delivery services:

Express Delivery – The customer will receive the purchased product on the same day. Purchases made after 15:00 will be delivered the next day. The delivery fee is 5 GEL, while for orders under 50 GEL, express delivery costs 10 GEL.

Standard Delivery – The customer will receive the purchased item for free within 3–5 business days.

Regional Delivery – Free delivery within 3–6 business days.

*For orders under 50 GEL, the standard delivery fee is 5 GEL. For orders above 50 GEL, the standard delivery service is free.
Deliveries across Georgia are handled by Georgian Post, while within Tbilisi deliveries are made by Glovo, Wolt, Unibox, BM’s, TNT, and Georgian Post.

**Unfortunately, express delivery is not available for the following areas: Lilo, Ponichala, Okrokhana, Airport, Avchala, Tsavkisi, Okrokhana, Tskneti.

***Courier service does not apply to special administrative units. These include Mestia, Stepantsminda, Dusheti, Tianeti, Tsageri, and Lentekhi. In such cases, the customer must collect the order from the local post office.
