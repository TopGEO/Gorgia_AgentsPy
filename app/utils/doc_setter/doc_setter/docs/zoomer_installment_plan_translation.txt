Installment Plan
How to purchase technology online with an installment plan at Zoommer?

You can use installment plans from TBC Bank, Bank of Georgia, and Credo Bank within the Zoommer network.
To make an online installment purchase on the Zoommer website, you first need to register or log in on the Zoommer website. See the instructions below:

Step 1: Register with your Google account or phone number and fill in your profile completely with your personal information. If you are already registered, log in with your email, Google account, or phone number.

Step 2: After registration, choose your desired product on the Zoommer website and click the “Buy” button.

Step 6: After choosing the bank, click “Next” to be redirected to the selected bank’s website.


Bank 1: TBC Bank

In the payment options, choose TBC Bank installment, click “Continue,” and proceed to the bank’s page. Enter your personal ID and mobile number, confirm you are not a robot, and input the verification code for security.

Fill out the application form: employment sector, income, participation, address, and contact person.

After entering your financial information, proceed to the confirmation page, where you can carefully read the bank’s terms and conditions and, if you agree, check the box “I agree with the terms.”

Upload your income verification document.

Once all procedures are completed successfully, wait for a call from our operator regarding product delivery or pickup. We will deliver your desired product, or you can pick it up from the selected branch.


Bank 2: Bank of Georgia

After selecting Bank of Georgia in the payment methods, you will be redirected to the bank’s website, where you will enter your data or register if necessary.

Review the Bank of Georgia’s terms and agree if you wish to continue.

Fill in your financial information in the required fields.

Choose your installment period.

You will receive an SMS confirmation regarding your installment approval.

Once all procedures are completed successfully, wait for a call from our operator regarding product delivery or pickup. We will deliver your desired product, or you can pick it up from the selected branch.


Bank 3: Credo Bank

When choosing Credo Bank installment, you will be redirected to the Credo Bank website, where your data will be automatically displayed by the bank. You can choose your preferred installment period.

Fill in your personal information.

Register on the bank’s website.

Review the terms and agree if you wish to proceed.

Complete the identification process.

Once all procedures are completed successfully, wait for a call from our operator regarding product delivery or pickup. We will deliver your desired product, or you can pick it up from the selected branch.
