Payment Methods - Gorgia Online Store

---
### Available Payment Options
Customers can pay for their orders using the following methods:
1. By Card (Visa, MasterCard, Amex)
2. By Bank Transfer
3. By Installment (through partner banks)
4. By Contract (based on agreement with the company)

---
