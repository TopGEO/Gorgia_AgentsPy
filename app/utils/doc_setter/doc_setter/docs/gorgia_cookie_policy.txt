### What are Cookies and Why We Use Them
Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your computer or mobile device during your visit.  
They help us manage and improve the performance of the website.  
Through cookies, we observe user behavior to personalize your experience and provide access to useful features.

---
### Types of Cookies We Use

**First-party cookies**  
Used directly by gorgia.ge.  
They are necessary to provide services requested by the user.

**Third-party cookies**  
Facilitate website functionality or are used for statistical purposes.  
We use Google Analytics, an easy-to-use tool that helps website owners collect statistical data about visitors.  
When Google Analytics is used, certain cookies are stored on your computer.  
The Google Analytics user identifier helps track statistics and is stored for 2 years.  
You can delete it anytime through your browser’s cookie settings.

**Verification Marker Cookie**  
Used to block unauthorized content submission (Cross-Site Request Forgery protection).  
This cookie is essential for safe website operation and does not collect user information.  
It is stored as a session cookie and deleted automatically when you close your browser.

---
### Cookie Categories

**Essential Cookies**  
These cookies make the website functional.  
They enable basic features such as navigation and access to secure areas.  
Without them, the website cannot function properly.

**Analytical Cookies**  
Collect information about how visitors use the website, such as which pages are visited most.  
LLC “Gorgia” uses these cookies to improve website performance.  
Some analytical cookies are third-party (for example, Google) and are used to collect user statistics.

**Functional Cookies**  
Remember user preferences, such as cookie consent, to avoid resetting them on each visit.

**Third-Party Cookies**  
Simplify website use or collect statistical data about users.  
They may allow login through services like Google or Facebook.

---
### How to Delete Cookies
You can delete cookies directly from your browser’s settings.  
However, deleting cookies may limit access to certain website features and negatively affect your browsing experience.
