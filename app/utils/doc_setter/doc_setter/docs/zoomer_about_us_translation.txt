15 Years of Zoommer's Development History

Zoommer has been operating in the digital technology market for 15 years. Over the years, the company has successfully established its place in retail sales and consistently maintains a leading position. Zoommer offers customers products from global brands at the best prices in Georgia.

The company entered the digital technology market in October 2009 with a single store. Today, it unites 18 branches and more than 400 employees across the country.

Zoommer is one of the largest retail networks for smartphones, digital cameras, laptops, and audio-video equipment, offering customers the lowest prices in Georgia.

It is noteworthy that the company is 100% Georgian investment and, 15 years ago, was a startup founded by young entrepreneurs, which over the years has evolved into a large retail network.
