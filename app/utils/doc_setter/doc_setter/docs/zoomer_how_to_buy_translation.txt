How to Purchase Technology at Zoommer?

Getting your desired tech from zoommer.ge is super easy!

Step 1: Register with your Google account or phone number and fill in your profile completely with your personal information. If you are already registered, log in using your email, Google account, or phone number.


Step 2: After registration, choose your desired item on the Zoommer website and click the “Buy” button.


Step 3: Choose the delivery method and type.


Step 4: Add the delivery address for your order.


Step 5: Almost done! Choose one of the delivery options – express or standard.


Step 6: It’s time to choose a payment method! You can pay by installment, card, points, or bank transfer. You can find instructions for installment applications at this link.


Step 7: Congratulations on your purchase! To view your order number, go to your profile page and select “Orders.”

Current Orders – here you can see all active orders that are still being processed.

Completed Orders – here you can view all completed or confirmed orders.
