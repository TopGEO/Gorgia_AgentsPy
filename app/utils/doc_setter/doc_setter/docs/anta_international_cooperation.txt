International Cooperation - ANTA Company

---
### Company Overview
ANTA is an international company established in 1998 with offices and warehouses in Georgia, Turkey, Uzbekistan, and Bulgaria.
The company’s core business involves the development and global distribution of building materials, household products, and equipment.

Key facts:
- Retail chains, regional distributors, and construction companies are the main partners.
- Total commercial area: 70,000 m²
- Warehouse area: 120,000 m²
- Product range: 50,000 items across various classifications
- Employees: 2,500 staff members
- Partnerships: Over 600 manufacturers worldwide

---
### Target Markets
- **Bulgaria Office** – Oversees Southeast Europe: Albania, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Greece, Kosovo, Montenegro, North Macedonia, Romania, and Serbia.
- **Georgia Office** – Oversees the Caucasus region: Armenia, Azerbaijan, Georgia, and parts of Southern Russia.
- **Uzbekistan Office** – Oversees Central Asia: Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, and Uzbekistan.
- **Turkey Office** – Oversees Southwest Asia.

---
### Objectives
Through extensive market experience and close connections with major business sectors, ANTA identified the need for a secure and attractive alternative for importers and businesses seeking transparency, insight, and real opportunities in global markets.
ANTA emerged as a producer, exporter, and strategic link connecting major investors to global market opportunities.
